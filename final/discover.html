The walking with lions is an adventurous activity proposed to those who wish to experience the wild side. Don't miss this unique opportunity which will be the highlight of your holiday in Mauritius! This activity takes place on the banks of Riviere du Rempart in a separate area of the Safari park.

Mauritius Attractions - Parks, Reserves and Sites

Nature Parks, Wildlife sanctuaries, Museums, Gardens, Aquariums and many more… Mauritius is spoilt with long list of great places to visit. So no matter what your preference is; if you are searching to unveil the culture of the island, discover the local history or see the amazing wildlife of Mauritius. The wide diversity of landscapes will never cease to amaze you and few of the best places to visit are the Safari and Bird Park, Vallée des Couleurs, Crocodile & Giant Tortoises Park and Ile Aux Aigrettes Island.

Visit of Mauritius Aquarium

The aquarium, located in a small relaxing village in the North West of Mauritius, is home to a large number of species including over 200 species of fish, invertebrates, live coral and sponges, originating from the waters around the island.
